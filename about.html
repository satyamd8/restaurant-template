Our Story

Welcome to Diso's, an authentic American casual dining experience located at the heart of the Big Apple

What started as two Italian brothers, making Italian subs from a street cart has now grown into a haven for the best classic American food with an Italian twist.
At Diso's, every single dish is hand-crafted with fresh, high-quality, and local produce, provided by our fellow New Yorkers.
We aim to be the standard American dining experience that everyone tries to be, one that brings people together from all over the world, enjoying the very best food.
